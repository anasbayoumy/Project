Privacy Policy

General information
DayCare collects personal information that you provide when creating an account, such as your name, email address, contact number, and any other details necessary for offering our services. For parents, this may include information about your child, such as age and special requirements, to help match you with the most suitable nursery. Nursery owners may provide business-related information, including location, services offered, and contact details, to improve visibility and reputation within the DayCare network.
Use of Information
The information collected is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. For parents, this includes helping you find nurseries that meet your specific needs. For nursery owners, it means expanding your reach and reputation among potential clients. DayCare may also use your information to communicate with you, including sending notifications, updates, and promotional offers related to our services.
Management of personal data
DayCare does not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Nursery-related information may be shared with parents to facilitate the matching process. We may also share aggregated, non-identifiable data with partners and advertisers for the purpose of improving our services.
Data security
DayCare employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. We regularly update our systems and processes to ensure that your data remains secure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.
User Rights
You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to make any changes, please contact our support team, and we will assist you promptly. You may also choose to deactivate your account, in which case your information will no longer be accessible through the DayCare app.
Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at dayccare@gmail.com. We are committed to addressing your concerns and ensuring that your experience with DayCare is safe and secure.
